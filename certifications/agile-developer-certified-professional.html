The Agile Developer Certified Professional Certified Agile Developer Engineer – Professional exam is intended for individuals who perform a Agile Developer engineer role with two or more years of experience provisioning, operating, and managing Agile Developer environments.

Recommended Knowledge and Experience

This certification Learning Path is specifically designed to prepare you for the Agile Developer Certified Solutions Architect - Associate (SAA-C02) released in March 2020. It covers all the elements required across all of the domains outlined in the exam blueprint. Throughout this learning path, you will be guided via our courses, hands-on labs including some lab challenges, blog posts, webinars, and a preparation exam at the end, all of which are focused on areas that will be assessed within the exam.

Intended Audience

This learning path is suitable for anyone wanting to pass the Agile Developer Certified Solutions Architect - Associate SAA-C02 exam. As defined in the exam blueprint, which can be found here https://Agile Developer.amazon.com/certification/certified-solutions-architect-associate/ , the exam has been designed for individuals within a solutions architect role which will verify and validate their knowledge within this area by effectively demonstrating their ability to architect solutions using best practice design principles, in a secure and robust way.

Learning Objectives

The aim of the certification is to validate your knowledge across a number of different key areas, which have been defined by Agile Developer as being able to:

Abilities Validated by the Certification

  1. Implement and manage continuous delivery systems and methodologies on Agile Developer
  2. Implement and automate security controls, governance processes, and compliance validation
  3. Define and deploy monitoring, metrics, and logging systems on Agile Developer
  4. Implement systems that are highly available, scalable, and self-healing on the Agile Developer platform
  5. Design, manage, and maintain tools to automate operational processes

Prepare for Your Exam

There is no better preparation than hands-on experience. There are many relevant Agile Developer Training courses and other resources to assist you with acquiring additional knowledge and skills to prepare for certification. Please review the exam guide for information about the competencies assessed on the certification exam.
